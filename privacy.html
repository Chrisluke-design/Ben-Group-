4. Refund Policy

4.1 Investment Commitments

All investment commitments made to BEN GROUP for real estate syndication, agricultural production, or strategic business investments are generally **non-refundable** once funds have been deployed into the specific project. This is due to the illiquid nature of real estate and pooled investment structures.

4.2 Exceptions for Refund

Refunds may be considered only under the following exceptional circumstances:

  • Project Cancellation: If BEN GROUP cancels an investment project before funds are deployed, a full refund of your committed capital will be issued within 14 business days.
  • Failure to Meet Project Minimums: If a project does not reach its minimum funding target, your invested capital will be fully refunded within 14 business days.
  • Material Breach by BEN GROUP: In the event of a proven material breach of the investment agreement by BEN GROUP that fundamentally impacts your investment, refunds will be handled on a case-by-case basis, subject to arbitration as outlined in our Terms and Conditions.

4.3 Refund Process

For eligible refunds, the process is as follows:

  • Notification: You will be notified by BEN GROUP regarding the eligibility of your refund.
  • Verification: We will verify your original investment details and payment method.
  • Processing: Refunds will be processed back to the original payment method or directly to your bank account (Fidelity Bank: Account Number: 5601450259, Account Name: Billionaire Estators Nation Limited) within the specified timeframe (typically 14 business days). Please note that processing times may vary based on your bank.

No Refunds for Investment Performance:

Losses incurred due to market fluctuations, poor investment performance, or any of the inherent risks disclosed in our investment memorandums are not eligible for a refund. Investors assume the risks associated with real estate and business investments.
